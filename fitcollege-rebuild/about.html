About us · RTO 31903

Twenty years of making trainers, in person

FIT College was formed with one goal: graduate quality personal trainers and gym instructors ready to work in any fitness environment. Two decades on, we're one of the last providers still teaching face-to-face at scale — 20 campuses nationally, backed by a staffed Virtual Campus.

What we stand behind

Our course content is authored in-house by our own sports science team — not bought off the shelf — and refined over 20 years of delivery. Our careers liaison team works with graduates and affiliate gyms across the country, because a qualification without a job isn't the outcome anyone enrolled for.

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

Already working in the industry, or returning after time away? RPL can credit your existing skills and experience toward a qualification, shortening your study. Talk to a Career Advisor about an RPL assessment — it's a dignified route back for experienced professionals re-qualifying.

Unique Student Identifier (USI)

All students in nationally recognised training need a USI. It's free, takes minutes at usi.gov.au, and we'll help you sort it at enrolment if you haven't already.
