Affiliate offer FAQ

When can I start?

FIT College offers 3 face-to-face intakes a year, starting in February, May and September. Check out the Campus Location Finder to look up timetables in your local area.

What are the qualifications?

SIS30321 Certificate III in Fitness — Fitness Coach, and SIS40221 Certificate IV in Fitness — Personal Trainer.

How long is the course?

The face-to-face modes are structured workshop delivery of either 14 weeks (full-time) or 28 weeks (part-time). For students who prefer flexibility, FIT College also offers online modes that are flexible.

What are the requirements?

The Certificate III in Fitness is classed as an "Entry Level" course; Certificate IV in Fitness requires a current Certificate III in Fitness or the minimum entry requirement as stated by Training.gov.au. You must be at least 18 years old (or if under 18, you'll need parent/guardian consent), provide a valid Unique Student Identifier (USI), and complete a Language, Literacy and Numeracy (LLN) indicator assessment as part of enrolment.
