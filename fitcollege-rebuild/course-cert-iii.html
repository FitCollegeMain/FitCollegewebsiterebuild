Certificate III in Fitness

Overview

The SIS30321 Certificate III in Fitness qualifies you to work as a gym instructor — planning and delivering group sessions and gym-based programs for the general population. It's the entry point to the industry and the prerequisite for the SIS40221 Certificate IV in Fitness (Personal Trainer). No prior experience is required.

What you'll learn

  • Anatomy, physiology and the science of exercise
  • Planning and instructing gym-based exercise
  • Delivering group exercise sessions
  • Health screening and fitness appraisals
  • Working safely and inclusively with diverse clients

Where it takes you

Gym floor instructor, group fitness roles, and the direct pathway to the Certificate IV and personal training.
