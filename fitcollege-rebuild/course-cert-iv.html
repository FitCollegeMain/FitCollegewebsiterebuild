Certificate IV in Fitness

Overview

The SIS40221 Certificate IV in Fitness is the nationally recognised personal trainer qualification. Building on the Certificate III, it qualifies you to work independently with clients — designing individualised programs, running your own PT business, and training a broader range of populations.

What you'll learn

  • Individualised program design and periodisation
  • Nutrition guidance within scope of practice
  • Working with older adults and specific population groups
  • Building and running a personal training business
  • Advanced exercise science and coaching

Where it takes you

Personal trainer in gyms and studios, independent PT business owner, and the platform for specialisation in strength & conditioning, coaching or education.

Certificate IV in Fitness FAQ

What's the prerequisite for the Cert IV?

The SIS30321 Certificate III in Fitness. Many students complete both through the FIT Elite™ dual-career program.

Can I run my own PT business with this qualification?

Yes — the Certificate IV is the industry-standard qualification for independent personal training, and business skills are part of the course.

How quickly are assessments marked?

Within five business days, in every study mode.
