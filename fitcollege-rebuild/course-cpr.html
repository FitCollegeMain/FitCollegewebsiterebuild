Overview

HLTAID009 Provide Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation is the nationally recognised CPR unit — the standard annual refresher for fitness professionals and workplace first aiders, delivered with hands-on practical assessment.

What you'll learn

  • Performing CPR on adults, children and infants
  • Using an automated external defibrillator
  • Responding within your role and responsibilities

Where it takes you

Maintains currency for personal trainers, gym instructors and workplace first aiders.
