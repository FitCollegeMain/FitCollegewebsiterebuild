Diploma of Sport

Overview

The SIS50321 Diploma of Sport (Coaching) takes qualified fitness professionals into coaching — developing athletes, managing programs and working in sporting environments. It's the natural next rung after the Certificate IV for graduates targeting sport rather than general population training.

What you'll learn

  • Coaching methodology and athlete development
  • Program management in sporting environments
  • Performance analysis fundamentals
  • Working within sporting organisations

Where it takes you

Coaching roles in clubs and sporting organisations, athlete development, and performance-focused training environments.
