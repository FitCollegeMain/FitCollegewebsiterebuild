Disability Skill Set

Overview

The CHCSS00130 Individual Support (Disability) Skill Set equips you to work in disability support roles — a growing sector with immediate demand for skilled, movement-literate workers. Available standalone or as the dual-career stream inside FIT Elite™, where it pairs paid support work with a growing personal training career.

What you'll learn

  • Providing individual support in disability settings
  • Facilitating the empowerment of people with disability
  • Following safe work practices for direct client care

Where it takes you

Individual support roles in the disability sector — and, combined with fitness qualifications, adaptive and inclusive exercise delivery.
