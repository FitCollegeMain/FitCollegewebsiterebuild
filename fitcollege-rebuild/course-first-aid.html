Overview

HLTAID011 Provide First Aid is the nationally recognised first aid unit required across the fitness industry and most workplaces. Delivered at FIT College campuses with hands-on practical assessment.

What you'll learn

  • Responding to emergency situations
  • CPR and defibrillation
  • Managing casualties until professional help arrives

Where it takes you

Required certification for personal trainers, gym instructors and most workplace roles.
