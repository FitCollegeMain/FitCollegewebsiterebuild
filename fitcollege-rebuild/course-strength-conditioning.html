Strength & Conditioning

Overview

FIT College's Strength & Conditioning course carries ASCA Level 1 accreditation from the Australian Strength and Conditioning Association — the recognised credential for coaches working in performance environments. Available standalone or as a FIT Elite™ specialty stream.

What you'll learn

  • Strength and power programming for performance
  • Speed, agility and conditioning methods
  • Testing and monitoring athletes
  • ASCA Level 1 accreditation on completion

Where it takes you

Performance coaching in team sport, athlete development programs, and S&C roles alongside personal training.

Strength & Conditioning FAQ

What is ASCA Level 1?

The entry accreditation of the Australian Strength and Conditioning Association — the recognised credential for S&C coaches in Australia.

Can I take this as part of FIT Elite?

Yes — Strength & Conditioning is one of the two FIT Elite™ specialty streams, alongside the Disability Skill Set.
