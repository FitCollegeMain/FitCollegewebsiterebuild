TAE Upgrade

Overview

The TAE40116 has been superseded by the TAE40122 Certificate IV in Training and Assessment. This upgrade lets current TAE40116 holders transition to the current standard — recognising the competencies you already hold, addressing any gaps through targeted training, and ensuring compliance with the updated training package.

How the upgrade works

  • Prepare your academic transcript and USI (Unique Student Identifier)
  • FIT College assessors complete an RPL / credit-transfer evaluation of your existing units
  • Assessors identify any gap units you need to complete
  • Finish the gap units online or through on-campus workshops

Who it's for

Current holders of the TAE40116 — whether completed fully or partially — who want their skills aligned to the current VET standard. Looking for the full qualification instead? See the TAE40122 Certificate IV in Training and Assessment.
