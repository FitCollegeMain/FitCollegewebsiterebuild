Training & Assessment

Overview

The TAE40122 Certificate IV in Training and Assessment qualifies experienced professionals to train and assess in the vocational education system — the pathway from fitness professional to educator. Among benchmarked fitness education providers, FIT College is the only one offering this rung.

What you'll learn

  • Designing and delivering training
  • Assessing competence against training packages
  • Working within the VET quality framework

Where it takes you

Trainer and assessor roles in RTOs, enterprise training, and fitness education.
