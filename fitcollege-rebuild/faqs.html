FAQs

Is FIT College a registered training organisation?

Yes — FIT College is RTO 31903, delivering nationally recognised training across fitness, disability support, first aid and training & assessment.

Can I study on campus?

Yes — face-to-face classes run at 20 campuses across QLD, NSW, VIC, SA, WA, the ACT and Tasmania.

Can I study online?

Yes — the Virtual Campus delivers the same qualifications online with staffed support, 7am–7pm, seven days a week.

When are the intakes?

Three fixed intakes each year: February, May and September. When an intake closes, the next opportunity is the following intake.

How quickly are assessments marked?

Within five business days, in every study mode.

Are there placement hours?

No mandatory placement hours — practical skills are built into course delivery.

What does online study actually involve?

Online study includes video-based practical assessments, and you'll recruit practice clients (often friends or family) for role-play sessions. It suits self-directed learners; if you prefer structure and in-person coaching, campus study removes those requirements. A Career Advisor can help you pick the right mode honestly.

Is government funding available?

Eligible Queensland students can access a Career Start co-contribution place for the SIS30321 Certificate III in Fitness — FIT College is an approved Skills Assure Supplier. See the funding page for the 60-second eligibility checker.

Are payment plans available?

Yes — flexible, interest-free payment plans are available across courses.

What is FIT Elite?

FIT Elite™ is the Dual-Career Personal Trainer Program: the Cert III and Cert IV in Fitness plus a CEC short course pack and a specialty stream — the Disability Skill Set or ASCA Level 1 Strength & Conditioning.

Can a personal trainer work in disability support?

Yes — with the CHCSS00130 Disability Skill Set, a qualified PT can also work in disability support, earning from week one while building a client base.

Are FIT College qualifications recognised overseas?

FIT College qualifications carry ICREPs portability for international registration.

How do I talk to a real person?

Call 1300 887 017 or book a Career Advisor conversation — the advisor call is the fastest way to get course, funding and intake questions answered together.
