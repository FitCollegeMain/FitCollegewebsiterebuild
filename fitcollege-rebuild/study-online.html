What online study actually involves

Online study works brilliantly for self-directed learners — and it has real requirements worth knowing before you enrol. You'll complete video-based practical assessments, and you'll recruit practice clients (usually friends or family) for role-play sessions. If you'd rather have in-person coaching and a classroom rhythm, campus study removes both requirements. We'd rather tell you now than surprise you in week three.

  • Reliable internet, a device with a camera, and space to film practical assessments
  • Willing practice clients for role-play sessions
  • Self-directed study habits — the Virtual Campus supports you, but you set the pace
